Master Flow — Personal Email Enrichment + Ad Targeting + Deal Exclusion

Clay → RocketReach → HubSpot → Salesforce · HubSpot Native Ad Audience Sync → Meta & Google & LinkedIn

v2.1 (HubSpot Native Only — No Stape) · March 2026 · Alternative Payments · Internal & Confidential

① Enrichment

Clay's full 32,277-contact table → RocketReach personal email lookup. Results written to personal_email in HubSpot. Not found → Phase 2 fallback (Datagma/Clearbit). Errors → retry next cycle.

② HubSpot + Salesforce

Clay writes personal_email, enrichment_status, audience_segment to HubSpot. Two-way sync propagates to Salesforce automatically. Deals sync back from SF → HubSpot for the deal gate.

③ Deal Gate

Binary decision: has a deal? NO → targeting lists. YES → permanent exclusion from all advertising. No lifecycle stage = defaults to acquisition.

④ HubSpot Lists

4 targeting lists (master + Named Accounts, MSP US, MSP Canada) and 1 master exclusion list + 3 subsets. All Active Lists — auto-update as contacts enrich or deals are created.

⑤ HubSpot Native Sync

Connect Meta + Google + LinkedIn in HubSpot Ads settings. Create audience syncs from each list. Both email + personal_email sent hashed SHA-256. Sync runs continuously, updates every few hours.

⑥ Ad Platforms

Meta, Google, LinkedIn each get targeting + exclusion audiences. Personal email lifts match rates (Meta: 50–70%, Google: 60–80%). Exclusion audiences mandatory on every campaign — non-negotiable.
