Master Flow — Personal Email Enrichment + Ad Targeting + Deal Exclusion

Clay → RocketReach → HubSpot → Salesforce · HubSpot Native Sync + Stape.io CAPI → Meta & Google & LinkedIn

v2.0 · March 2026 · Alternative Payments · Internal & Confidential

① Enrichment

Clay's full 32,277-contact table sends each contact through RocketReach (First Name + Last Name + Work Domain) to find personal emails. Results written to personal_email in HubSpot. Not found contacts flagged for Phase 2 fallback (Datagma/Clearbit).

② HubSpot + Salesforce

Clay writes enriched data to HubSpot: personal_email, enrichment_status, enriched_at, audience_segment. Two-way sync auto-propagates to Salesforce — BDRs see personal email in SF. Deals sync back from SF → HubSpot.

③ Deal Gate

Single binary decision: does the contact have ANY deal association? NO → enters targeting lists. YES → permanently excluded from all advertising. Exclusion is permanent regardless of deal stage or outcome.

④ List Assignment

4 targeting lists (master + Named Accounts, MSP US, MSP Canada). 1 master exclusion list + 3 reporting subsets. Contacts with no lifecycle stage default to acquisition. SQL/Opportunity contacts: CRM only, no ads.

⑤ Dual Sync

Flow A (HubSpot Native): list-based, continuous, authoritative. Flow B (Stape CAPI): real-time events, closes sync delay gap, feeds platform algorithms. Both send hashed work + personal email.

⑥ Ad Platforms

Meta, Google, and LinkedIn each receive targeting + exclusion audiences. Personal email lifts match rates dramatically (Meta: 15–30% → 50–70%, Google: 30–50% → 60–80%). Exclusion audiences mandatory on every campaign.
