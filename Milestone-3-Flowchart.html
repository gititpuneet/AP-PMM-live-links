Avg MRR: $335 | 105 deals | Highest volume
Vulnerability: No CC fee pass-through (#1 switch reason), broken autopay, ACH fees rising to 1.75%, no client portal.

Avg MRR: $473 | 47 deals
Vulnerability: Low portal adoption rates. Setup friction blocks end-clients from paying.
"WisePay adoption rates are typically lower than expected. We've seen MSPs jump from 20% to 70%+ portal usage after switching."
Stripe
Avg MRR: $368 | 27 deals
Vulnerability: Built for transactions, not B2B AR. No portal, no collections, per-transaction pricing scales badly.
"Stripe charges per-transaction. At $50K/month volume, our flat subscription saves most MSPs 30-50% vs. Stripe's percentage model."
Manual / Checks
Avg MRR: $372 | 44 deals | Greenfield
Opportunity: 60-80% of clients move to digital in 90 days when given a portal. Easiest to convert — no competitor lock-in.
"What percentage of your clients pay online vs. by check today? MSPs that add a client portal see 60-80% digital adoption in 90 days."

Reply Handling Flow
A reply at any stage immediately pauses the sequence and routes through this flow.

Positive Reply

Route: Assigned AE inbox within 5 minutes via Slack
Response SLA: Within 2 hours
Template: "Thanks for getting back to me. Would you be open to a 20-minute call this week? No slides, no pitch. Just want to understand your world and see if there's a fit."
Goal: Book a demo within 48 hours of reply

Neutral / Question Reply

Route: ABM drafts response, AE personalizes and sends
Response SLA: Within 4 hours
Strategy: Answer the question directly, then pivot to discovery. "Great question — [answer]. Mind if I ask what prompted the question?"
Goal: Convert to positive engagement within 2 exchanges

Objection Reply

6 documented objections with response templates:
1. "Not a priority" → Ask what IS priority, position for future
2. "Already have something" → Probe for hidden pain
3. "Send me an email" → Ask what to personalize
4. "Clients pay fine" → Pivot to efficiency, not bad payers
5. "Not looking at tools" → Satisfied or bad timing?
6. "Not the DM" → Get DM name, ask for their perspective

Negative / Unsubscribe Reply

Action: Immediately stop sequence. No exceptions.
Response: "Understood, {{firstName}}. Removing you now. If anything changes, I'm easy to find. Appreciate your time."
CRM: Log as "Opted Out — Cold Outreach" with date
Cooldown: 6 months minimum before any re-engagement

Out of Office

Action: Pause sequence automatically
Resume: 3 days after stated return date
Note: If OOO mentions a colleague, do NOT redirect. Wait for the original contact.

Speed-to-Lead Targets

Positive reply → AE notification: < 5 minutes (Slack automation)
AE notification → First response: < 2 hours
Positive reply → Demo booked: < 48 hours
Demo booked → Demo held: < 7 days
Speed-to-lead is the #1 predictor of conversion from reply to demo.
